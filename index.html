FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

  • Can you tell me more about your design process?

    After the deposit of 50% has been paid, you will receive the email confirming the payment and a welcome letter explaining the next steps. Here, you can see the details about the timeline and all the steps of the project, along with the contract.

  • How long does the work take?

    After we have agreed on the creative direction and I received all the logo and packaging files, It usually takes up to 7 business days for product rendering work and up to 14 for motion graphics, exluding the revision time. The timeline of the project will depend on the scope of work, so please contact me first to find out the details.

  • Is it true that you don't need the physical products for your work?

    Yes, that's right! To recreate your product in 3D, I need the photo from side and front as well as your logo design file. if the product is under development and you don't have it at hand yet, the packaging design file will suffice.

  • What if I don't have a creative strategy and don't know exactly what I want to see in the final images?

    If you are not sure about how to get the best visual results and what kind of visuals would help you achieve your goals, you can order the development of the creative visual direction including the image references and creative strategy. The creative direction is calculated separately from the renderings. Please tell me a couple of words about your brand and the product range in the contact form to get a more detailed quote.

  • What is 3D and how does it work?

    3D modeling / CGI - это процесс создания трехмерных цифровых моделей объектов или сцен. В 3D моделировании создается виртуальное трехмерное представление реальных объектов. 3D используется для создания прототипов продуктов и машин, моделируются продукты, упаковки и сцены для создания реалистичных и привлекательных рекламных материалов, баннеров, видеороликов и обложек. Это позволяет представить продукты в наилучшем свете и привлечь внимание потенциальных покупателей.
